This tool helps you to map out your career journey so far within the Coty family.


There are three stages to your lifetime career:

Early – 0 to 10 years

Mid – 11 to 20 years

Later – 20 years and onwards


To start, enter your name and click Start. Once you have started, do not refresh your browser as your progress will not be saved.


You can learn more about each experience by hovering over the title for that section.


To add a section of experience, click on the relevant segment and add the relevant Job Title and Company. This will be added to the career wheel and record list below. You can use the same job role to complete multiple areas of experience across the wheel.


You can delete an entry at any time by clicking on the X in the record list.


Once you are happy with your selections, click Print to save as a PDF or print a paper copy.
